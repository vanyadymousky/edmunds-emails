Today I would like to remind you about importance of using componentWillUnmount component lifecycle hook. From docs: componentWillUnmount() is invoked immediately before a component is unmounted and destroyed and it's usually used for any necessary cleanup, such as cleaning up any subscriptions that were created in componentDidMount().

Our carousel shared component can be good example, so having addEventListener subscription in componentDidMount

Should be supported by componentWillUnmount lifecycle hook with removeEventListener, cleaning listeners when component is removed/destroyed:

This brings performance gains when SPA functionality is activated in Venom.

Let's keep our codebase clean, tidy and don't reinvent wheels! Have a good day!
